## Progressive Attendance Application — London School of Commerce Group of Colleges

### Built with a Django and PostgreSQL backend, a React and TypeScript frontend styled with Tailwind CSS.

Built for a student body of 20,000, this system replaced the impractical manual process of taking attendance in classes of 150–200 students. Lecturers display a rotating barcode that refreshes every 10ms — fast enough to prevent sharing outside the room — while students scan it via a mobile app to register their presence instantly.

## Assessment & English Test Platform with Online Proctoring

### Built with React and TypeScript on the frontend, Flask and PostgreSQL on the backend, styled with Tailwind CSS.

An end-to-end platform for conducting assessments and English language tests online. Integrated proctoring verifies each student's identity at login and monitors them continuously via webcam throughout the exam.

## Virtual Learning Environment — London School of Commerce Group of Colleges

### Built on Moodle using PHP, MySQL, JavaScript, and HTML/CSS, with Composer and Node.js (Grunt/Babel) for dependency and frontend build management.

A fully automated academic workflow built during the pandemic, covering timetable management, Zoom link generation and distribution, attendance tracking, and assessments — end to end. Now the operational backbone for all LSC campuses across London, Bangladesh, Sri Lanka, Malta, and Malaysia.

## Global Accounts Portal

### Built with Python, Django, and Django REST Framework, backed by PostgreSQL and AWS (S3/SES), with Pandas, NumPy, and CKEditor for data processing and rich content management.

A centralised finance hub used by 30+ accountants to manage fee records and account details for approximately 30,000 students across every global campus. Originally written in Visual Basic in 2003, the system was fully modernised and migrated to AWS.

## Connexons Travel (India) Pvt. Ltd.

### Built with PHP, MySQL, and jQuery.

Website and tour package management system for Connexons Travel, an Indian tourism operator founded in October 1995. Headquartered in New Delhi with major offices in Mumbai and Chennai, and a network of 30+ representative and agency offices spanning India and Nepal. The platform supports their full range of services: cultural tours, beach holidays, educational trips, adventure travel, and bespoke itineraries.
